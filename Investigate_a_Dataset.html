Conclusions¶

This was a very interesting data analysis. I come out with resulting some facts about movies. after this analysis process I can take conclusion that:

  1. I concluded that all of budget, popoularity, vote rating have a positive impact on revenue
  2. The movies with higher runtime received better vote average.
  3. The popular has no correlation with The vote average.
  4. The popularity increased over years.
